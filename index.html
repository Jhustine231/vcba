About the school

Villamor College of Business and Arts

Villamor College of Business and Arts (VCBA) is a private educational institution owned by Eduardo Villamor. It was established on 2012 and is located at Abdawa Street, General Santos City. At present, VCBA offers degree programs in Business, Information System, Communication, and Criminology. Its Senior High School department provides the Academic and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) tracks, particularly the ABM, GAS, HUMSS, HE, and IA strands.
